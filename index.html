About Me

Hi! My name is Azdeen Jeljalane and I am a first generation Moroccan American with the hopes of learning more about the mystery that are computers. I have an interest in every aspect of software development that started after my first coding class in highschool. Since I was a young boy, I've always had an affinity towards technology, coding, and gaming. Some of the best memories I have ended with a broken gameboy and an apology to my mother. There will always be something intrinsically fascinating about what you can accomplish typing onto a mechanical keyboard and text appearing on the screen. Furthermore, learning software development from front to back end, databases and cloud, and even to project management is very rewarding and I hope that I continue to learn and improve on my skills.
